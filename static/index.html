Step 1: Extract faces from videos

Extract frames and faces from videos. Each video filename should be the student's name. After processing, you'll need to create a gallery from the extracted data.
How It Works
  1. Select the batch year and department for the students
  2. Provide the directory path where your student videos are stored
  3. Click "Process Video Frames" to extract faces
  4. Once complete, proceed to "Create Galleries" to build a face recognition gallery
